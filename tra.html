Traitors Castle Edition

How to Play

The Goal

  • The Faithful: You must identify and Banish all Traitors. You win if only Faithful remain.
  • The Traitors: You must remain undetected and Murder the Faithful at night. You win immediately if you equal or outnumber the Faithful.

Setup

Supports up to 20 players. The number of Traitors is randomized and unknown to the Faithful.

Voting Ties

If the banishment vote is a tie, a Revote is triggered immediately between the tied players. If it ties again, no one leaves.

The Night Phase

Everyone handles the device to hide identities.

  • Faithful: You will see a "Night Watch" screen. Hold the device for a moment to mimic Traitor thinking time, then pass it on.
  • Traitors: See the shared Log and vote to Murder.
  • Disagreement: If Traitors tie on a victim, the device must be passed around again until a consensus is reached.
