Traitors Castle Edition

How to Play

The Goal

  • The Faithful: You must identify and Banish all Traitors. You win if only Faithful remain.
  • The Traitors: You must remain undetected and Murder the Faithful at night. You win if you equal or outnumber the Faithful.

Setup

Supports up to 20 players.

  • 4-6 Players: 1 Traitor
  • 7-9 Players: 2 Traitors
  • 10-13 Players: 3 Traitors
  • 14+ Players: 4 Traitors

The Night Phase (Conspiracy)

Everyone handles the device to hide identities.

  • Faithful: See a sleeping screen.
  • Traitors: See a shared Log. You can see what other traitors voted for and leave notes.
  • Disagreement: If Traitors tie on a victim, the device must be passed around again until a consensus is reached.

The Loop

  1. Free Talk: 5 mins to mingle/whisper.
  2. Round Table: 3 mins to accuse publicly.
  3. Voting: Everyone votes secretly on who to Banish.
  4. Night: Traitors conspire and murder.
