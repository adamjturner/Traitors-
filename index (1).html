Traitors Castle Edition

How to Play

The Goal

  • The Faithful: You must identify and Banish all Traitors. You win if only Faithful remain.
  • The Traitors: You must remain undetected and Murder the Faithful at night. You win if you equal or outnumber the Faithful.

The Night Phase

To keep identities secret, everyone handles the device at night.

  • If Faithful: You will see a sleeping screen. Just tap continue.
  • If Traitor: You will select a victim. The person with the most Traitor votes dies.

The Loop

  1. Round Table: Discuss theories. Accuse people. Lie.
  2. Voting: Everyone votes secretly on who to Banish.
  3. Night: Traitors secretly murder one player.
